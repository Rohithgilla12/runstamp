# Runstamp — Content Calendar

> Week-by-week content schedule across Twitter/X, Instagram Stories, and
> blog. Mirrors `PLAN.md`. Format: `DAY — [CHANNEL] — POST TITLE`.
> Times are IST (UTC+5:30); the runner audience overlaps decently with
> US/EU evening posting on top of India morning.
>
> _Last updated 2026-05-16._

## Cadence rules

- **Mon / Wed / Fri** — Twitter post (artifact, build-log, or technical
  deep-dive — pick one tag per post).
- **Tue / Thu** — Instagram Story (vertical share-card or in-app moment).
- **Sun** — "Sunday share card" — feature one real user's stamp (with
  permission, see PLAN §4 Phase 2) across X + IG.
- **Monthly drop** — last Sunday of the month, "Month in Stamps" aggregate.
